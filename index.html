Travel in Russia

The real country is not in the news, but here.

What didn't we see?

According to VTsIOM polls, 95% of Russians dream of going somewhere, but only 36% planning to take a vacation native country. Like, what have we not seen here at home? In fact, Russia is a whole universe with the gentle sea of the south, the dense forests of the Sayans and the harsh ice of the Putorana Plateau. And see all these beauty is possible without millions in the account, a passport and many hours of flights. Such as, Faith Bashmakova is a brave young mother who took three children in an armful, put them in her Lada and traveled 20 thousand kilometers in his native country. We have selected and described some interesting places, worthy of your vacation.

Altai

URL

Facebook

Altai is one of the most beautiful places in Russia. First of all, because of the mountains: if you drive along the ridge, you will see slopes strewn with pine trees, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open the windows in the car, you can get acquainted with the invisible by the miracle of these places - mountain air.

The climate in Altai is moderate, so it is best to go here in summer. So you see everything diversity local flora and fauna. Moose roam the forests of Altai, eagles fly over the ridges, and on the plains deer graze. And the famous manuls are also inhabitants of the Altai Territory.

Karelia

Siberia ends not in the Urals, but in Karelia: the Siberian larch that forms the taiga does not grows west of Vodlozero. But here it towers up to 30 meters - the forests of the Karelian national parks because of the impenetrable swamps, the ax was never known. Some pines are already more than half a millennium. Touch a living creature that saw the sun before Ivan saw it Grozny. In the virgin forest for a hundred kilometers you will not find a path. And on rare paths trees a couple of meters from the ground are marked with bear claws. Let everyone know who's boss.
